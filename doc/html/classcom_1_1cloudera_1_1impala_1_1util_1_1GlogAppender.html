Manually override Log4j root logger configuration. Any values in log4j.properties not overridden (that is, anything but the root logger and its default level) will continue to have effect.

  • impalaLogLevel - the maximum log level for com.cloudera.impala.* classes
  • otherLogLevel - the maximum log level for all other classes
